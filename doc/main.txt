About tumorcode
==========
In 2006 D.- S. Lee, Heiko Rieger and K. Bartha started with 
computer simulations focusing on vascular remodeling in
growing solid tumors. Since than this code was continously 
improved and maintained by employees of Prof. Rieger.
In the meantime numerous publication followed und 
the code in now in the public domain.
